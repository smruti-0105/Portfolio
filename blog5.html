INTERNAL SMART INDIA HACKTHON


Our institute organised a Internal smart india hackthon where we were 6 member in it.The topic was in Eletrica-innovation. There had a gps system to track the car where it located.It has both hardware and software part in it.We approached with the chief with our idea and they said it was a great idea and keep work on it.
